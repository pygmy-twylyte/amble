The commands below are a partial list covering those most common. Many other actions are understood by the game and may be necessary to solve the puzzles, and you'll need to discover those for yourself!

Some items can be read or examined more closely (e.g. 'examine mug', 'read the security log') and provide extra detail, instructions for use, and other goodies.

You can talk to NPCs. Their moods and actions change depending on your game progress. Sometimes you can give them items, or take items from them.

Item names, character names, and directions are pattern-matched, so these all work (barring conflicting names):
    » talk to the black knight
    » talk to knight
    » talk to ack
    » talk to bl
